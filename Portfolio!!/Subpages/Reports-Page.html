Reports Page Redesign

Purpose: Create a Reports Page from the Perspective of a Patient

First

Patients need encouragement in times of struggle. Our application tracks improvements, so Sallie should be on the page encouraging patients and showing them specifically where they have improved.

Second

Patients already know what bothers them the most, but it is helpful to visually see it in a list style.

Third

Patients may be overwhelmed at a 30 Day Review of their symptoms, so Sallie should give them a chance to look at a 7 Day Review.
